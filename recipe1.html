Paneer Chilli

Paneer Chilli is a delicious Indo-Chinese dish where paneer (cottage cheese) is sautéed with colorful bell peppers and onions in a flavorful sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chilli sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Sauté ginger-garlic paste until aromatic.
  2. Add onions and bell peppers. Cook until they are slightly tender.
  3. Add paneer cubes and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Mix soy sauce, chilli sauce, ketchup, black pepper, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Cook for a few more minutes until the paneer is well coated with the sauce.
  6. Paneer Chilli is ready to be served. Garnish with spring onions and enjoy!
